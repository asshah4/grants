Summary of Research Efforts

This serves as a summary of current research progress, updates, and projects (clinical, translational, and programming). The most recent update to this page was as of Wednesday, November 16, 2022. Please see here for weekly updates.

The major research focuses are currently on:

  1. Stress and Cardiovascular Epidemiology: Physiological and psychological stress and cardiovascular mortality, from a neurocardiac perspective.
  2. Stress and Arrhythmogenesis: Mechanisms behind stress and arrhythmia generation (or degeneration) in a pre-clinical and clinical electrophysiology context.
  3. Computational Neurocardiology and Biostatistics: Programming-based approaches in signal processing and biostatistics.
  4. Clinical Projects: Work being done as a cardiology fellow at UIC/JBVA.
